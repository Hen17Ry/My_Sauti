Frequently Asked Questions

If you have questions about My Sauti, find the answers below. For further inquiries, feel free to contact us.

What is My Sauti?

My Sauti is an AI-powered communication platform designed to provide accessible and inclusive communication tools for people with hearing loss. It includes features like real-time sign language translation, a mobile app for converting signs to text and vice versa, and multi-platform integration.

How does the sign language translation work?

Our platform uses advanced AI and 3D animation technology to create avatars that translate spoken content into sign language in real-time. This ensures that users can understand and engage with audio content seamlessly.

Is My Sauti available on mobile devices?

Yes, My Sauti is available as a mobile application that allows users to convert signs to text and text to signs, facilitating easy and efficient communication on the go.

Can I integrate My Sauti with other platforms?

Absolutely! My Sauti offers a plugin that can be integrated into any platform offering audiovisual content, such as e-learning platforms, video conferencing applications, and TV channels.

How secure is My Sauti?

We take user privacy and data security very seriously. Our platform employs state-of-the-art security measures to protect user data and ensure a safe communication environment.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes, we provide dedicated customer support to assist with any questions or issues you may have. Our support team is available to help you make the most of My Sauti's features.

Are there different subscription plans available?

Yes, we offer various subscription plans to meet the diverse needs of our customers. From basic to premium and enterprise packages, we provide flexible options to suit different requirements.

How often is the platform updated?

We regularly update My Sauti to add new features, support additional languages, and improve overall functionality. We are committed to providing a cutting-edge communication tool for our users.

Can My Sauti be customized for my organization?

Yes, we offer customization options, including the development of custom avatars and advanced analytics, to enhance the user experience and meet the specific needs of your organization.

How does My Sauti support multiple languages and dialects?

My Sauti is designed to support multiple languages and dialects, ensuring that users from diverse linguistic backgrounds can access and benefit from our services. We continuously work with linguists and sign language experts to expand and refine our language database.
